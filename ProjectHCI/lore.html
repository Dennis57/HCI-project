The Lore

In the Kingdom of Swordonia, there lies a special group of people who are able to wield the sword with incredible skills, they are called Chasers. Every 4 years, there is a tournament to determine the strongest Chaser. Chasers will fight each other and the winner will own the title of Xtreme Chaser.

Enter Kirito and Eugeo, two kids from a village way outside the outskirts of Swordonia. During their time in the village they have been trained by their Kirito's father in the way of the sword. They enjoyed their time learning and training but suddenly Kirito's father had gone missing and the only clues were pointing towards the Xtreme Chaser tournament. Eager to face strong opponents and surprise the competition with something they have never seen before and whilst finding clues about their father and mentor. Kirito, Eugeo, and the other Chasers are looking forward to showing their skills and getting the title of Xtreme Chaser.
